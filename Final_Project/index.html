About Me


My name is Tyana Stone and I got my start on my journey to couponing a couple of months ago. Growing up I was always bargain shopping with my mother and as I got older, I started finding ways to save at restaurants and different retail stores by signing up for many email clubs. After having my son and becoming a stay at home single mother I realized that I needed to save bigger and that's when I started investing into extreme couponing.
